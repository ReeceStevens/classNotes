Preparing for a presentation:
    Organize your ideas for impact, clarity, and retention
        Chunk speech into 2-5 main points
        Retention structure:
            1. Tell them what you're going to tell them
            2. Tell them
            3. Tell them what you've just told them
    Preparing notes:
        presentation outline
        speaking outline (one single notecard, keywords)
    Practice the ENTIRE presentation
